About me

Hello and welcome to my page!

My name is Panayiota Sketsiou and I have recently completed my Ph.D. in geophysics and seismology. I find it a fascinating subject, and something that a lot of people and children would be interested to know more about, so I created this page which provides basic information on earthquakes, as well as how earthquakes can be useful and provide information about the Earth.

I am currently working as a consultant, but outside of work I enjoy baking for my friends and family, cooking, and learning.

I hope you find the content useful, and enjoy your browsing!
